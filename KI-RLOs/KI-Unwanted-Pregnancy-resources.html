Unwanted Pregnancy

10. Resources

Please select any of the folllowing buttons to view further information related to this learning resource.

Glossary

TermDefinition
Contraception

A method that prevents pregnancy. This can include birth control pills, IUDs, condoms, etc.

Abortion

A method to stop a pregnancy.

Resources

Resource Title
Centre for Reproductive Rights: European Abortion Law

Learning outcomes

By completing this resource you will be able to:

  • Understand the general perspective in the European Union regarding unwanted pregnancies.
  • Be aware of the services that may be available to you regarding contraceptives and abortions.
